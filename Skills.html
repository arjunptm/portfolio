I love staying keeping my imagination running wild, and also have the skills required to create them digitally. I sketch on any platform I can find, be it a friend's iPad, my trusty Note 9, or even complete digital tools such as Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. The unique feel of a drawing tablet appeals to me as much as paper and markers do. This skill also augments my game-dev work as I get to formulate and visualize my characters, NPCs, and game world objects.
