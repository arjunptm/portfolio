Esports and HCI Researcher - PhD Candidate at NC State

Exploring how competition shapes performance in games and beyond.

Hi - I'm Arjun. I study competitive play as a research setting: how incentives, expertise, and pressure change what players perceive and how they perform. I build and run tournament-experiments - controlled studies embedded inside real esports competitions.
