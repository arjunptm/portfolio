Beyond Research

A few non-academic interests that keep me grounded and curious.

This page is intentionally lightweight - it gives collaborators and students a glimpse of what I do when I'm not writing papers or running experiments. If you prefer an academic-only view, you can safely ignore this page.

Art

I make digital and traditional art sketches. Some recent work is on Instagram: @arjunmakes.

Making and tinkering

  • Woodworking and small DIY projects (fixtures, organizers, small gifts).
  • Game and interaction prototypes - quick experiments for learning, not always polished products.

Games, music, and travel

  • I enjoy competitive and cooperative games (esports and board games).
  • I explore music traditions from around the world.
  • I like road trips and geography trivia - it keeps long drives fun.
